Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of crushed biscuits
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) of softened cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1/4 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruits for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the crushed biscuits with melted butter and press the mixture into the base of a cheesecake pan.
  3. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then set it aside to cool.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese with sugar and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Pour the mixture over the baked crust and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake the cheesecake for 55-60 minutes or until the center is set.
  8. Mix the sour cream with powdered sugar and vanilla, then pour it over the baked cheesecake. Return it to the oven for an additional 10 minutes.
  9. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours before serving.
  10. Garnish with fresh fruits before serving (optional).
