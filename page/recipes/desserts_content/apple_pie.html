Apple Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups of flour.
  2. 1 cup of sugar.
  3. 2 eggs.
  4. 1/2 cup of melted butter.
  5. 1/2 cup of milk.
  6. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  7. 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon.
  8. 2 medium apples, sliced.
  9. 1 teaspoon of baking powder.

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix sugar and butter until the mixture is creamy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, then mix well.
  4. Gradually add flour, baking powder, and cinnamon, alternating with milk, until you get a smooth batter.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and evenly arrange the apple slices on top.
  6. Bake the cake for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Let the cake cool slightly before serving.
