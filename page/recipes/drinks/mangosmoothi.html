How to Make Mango Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of fresh or frozen mango chunks.
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt.
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice.
  • 2 teaspoons of honey (optional).
  • Ice cubes (optional).

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a blender, add the mango chunks and orange juice.
  2. Add the yogurt and honey if you prefer more sweetness.
  3. Blend the ingredients well until you get a smooth and creamy texture.
  4. Add ice cubes if you want a more refreshing drink.
  5. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
