Classic Latte Recipe

Ingredients

  • One or two shots of espresso
  • 180 ml of milk
  • Milk foam
  • (Optional) Cocoa powder or cinnamon for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prepare the espresso using a coffee machine.
  2. Heat the milk to 60-70°C (140-158°F).
  3. Use a steaming wand or milk frother to create foam.
  4. Pour the espresso into a serving cup.
  5. Slowly add the steamed milk over the espresso.
  6. Top with a layer of foam.
  7. Sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon on top (optional).
