How to Make Chocolate Mocha

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons of instant coffee powder.
  • 1 cup of hot milk.
  • 2 teaspoons of cocoa powder.
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar (optional, to taste).
  • Whipped cream for garnish (optional).
  • Grated chocolate for garnish (optional).

Preparation Steps:

  1. In a cup, mix the instant coffee powder with the cocoa powder and sugar.
  2. Add a small amount of hot milk and stir well to create a smooth mixture.
  3. Gradually pour in the remaining hot milk while stirring continuously.
  4. Garnish with whipped cream and grated chocolate if desired.
  5. Serve the mocha hot and enjoy its delicious taste!
